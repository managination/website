## Our Solution: Rule-Based, Privacy-First Messaging

Imagine a messaging system where:
- **You control what you see** with simple, powerful settings
- **Businesses reach the right audience** without knowing who they are
- **Your personal information stays private** on your device, not in company databases

Your phone checks if you qualify <em>locally</em>, without telling anyone. If you match, you see the message. If not, you don'tâ€”and the business never knows either way.</p>
